Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your DARE account and associated data, you can do so directly within the app.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the DARE app
  2. Go to your Profile
  3. Open Settings
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

Data that will be deleted

  • Your user account
  • Your profile information
  • Your created content
  • Your activity associated with the account

Data retention

Some technical logs may be retained for a limited time for security and fraud prevention purposes.
